About War of Sticks

War of Sticks is a highly engaging stickman fighting game that brings intense combat action to your screen. With its simple controls and deep combat system, it offers hours of entertainment for both casual players and fighting game enthusiasts.

Multiple Characters

Choose from a variety of stickman warriors, each with unique abilities and fighting styles. Master each character to find your perfect combat style.

Epic Battles

Engage in intense battles across various arenas. Use combos, special moves, and strategic thinking to defeat your opponents.

Progression System

Upgrade your characters, unlock new abilities, and collect powerful weapons to become the ultimate stickman warrior.

Game Tips & Strategies

Mastering War of Sticks requires practice and strategy. Here are some tips to help you become a better fighter:

  • Learn combo sequences for your favorite characters to maximize damage
  • Use dodge effectively to avoid enemy attacks and counter
  • Upgrade your weapons regularly to gain combat advantages
  • Practice in single-player mode before challenging other players
